# News Monitoring Watchlist (Sample)

Themes and example angles the agent should track for RTA. Use these to drive
`monitor_topic` scans and to judge what's relevant.

## 1. Road safety & regulation
- New traffic laws, fines, and enforcement changes (UAE/GCC).
- Speed-management, seatbelt, distracted-driving initiatives.
- Vision-Zero-style road-safety strategies.

## 2. Smart mobility & ITS
- Intelligent transport systems, connected corridors, traffic AI.
- Mobility-as-a-Service (MaaS), integrated ticketing.
- Smart-city transport projects in the region.

## 3. Autonomous & connected vehicles
- AV pilots, robotaxis, regulation and safety records.
- V2X / connected-vehicle standards.

## 4. Public transport & infrastructure
- Metro, bus, marine transport expansions and ridership.
- Major road/infrastructure projects and openings.

## 5. Electrification & sustainability
- EV adoption, charging networks, fleet electrification.
- Emissions and sustainable-transport policy.

## 6. Technology & data
- AI in transport operations and planning.
- Data-sharing, privacy, and cybersecurity for mobility data.

## How to use
- For a weekly brief, run `monitor_topic` on each active theme with a few angles
  (e.g. "regulation", "technology", "investment").
- Prioritise items relevant to the **UAE and GCC**, then global items that set a
  precedent.
- Flag anything with direct regulatory or operational impact on RTA.
