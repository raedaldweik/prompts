# News Monitoring Watchlist (Sample)

Themes and example angles the agent should track for NCGR. Use these to drive
`monitor_topic` scans and to judge what's relevant.

## 1. Government procurement & regulation (KSA)
- Changes to the Government Tenders and Procurement Law or its Executive
  Regulations; new Ministry of Finance circulars.
- Etimad platform announcements: new services, digital contracting,
  integration milestones.
- Local content and SME-participation rules in government procurement
  (LCGPA developments).

## 2. Procurement integrity & anti-corruption
- Nazaha (Oversight and Anti-Corruption Authority) announcements and cases
  touching public procurement.
- International guidance updates: OECD bid-rigging/procurement
  recommendations, World Bank debarments of suppliers active in the region.

## 3. Suppliers & markets
- Major government contract awards and the suppliers winning them.
- Supplier financial distress, mergers, or sanctions that could affect
  government contracts.
- Price movements in heavily procured categories (construction materials,
  IT, medical supplies).

## 4. Government digital transformation
- GovTech and e-procurement initiatives in KSA and the GCC.
- AI in government: policies, platforms, notable deployments (especially in
  finance/procurement functions).
- Vision 2030 programs touching government resource systems.

## 5. Economy & budget
- Saudi budget announcements and spending priorities (they drive procurement
  volumes).
- Inflation/supply-chain signals relevant to government purchasing power.
