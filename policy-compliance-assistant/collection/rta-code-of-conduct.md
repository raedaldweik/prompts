# RTA Employee Code of Conduct (Sample)

**Document ID:** HR-COC-003 · **Version:** 1.2 · **Owner:** Human Resources · **Effective:** 2026-01-01

## 1. Purpose
To set the standards of professional and ethical behaviour expected of every RTA employee.

## 2. Integrity & Anti-Bribery
2.1 Employees **must not** solicit or accept bribes, kickbacks, or any improper advantage.
2.2 Gifts and hospitality from suppliers or customers are **prohibited** unless they are of nominal value (AED 200 or less) and declared to the line manager.
2.3 Facilitation payments are prohibited.

## 3. Conflicts of Interest
3.1 Employees **must** declare any personal, family, or financial interest that could conflict with their duties.
3.2 Employees may not award, influence, or supervise contracts involving a family member or a business they have an interest in.

## 4. Use of RTA Resources
4.1 RTA assets, systems, and data are for official use; incidental personal use must be reasonable and lawful.
4.2 Confidential information **must not** be disclosed during or after employment without authorization.

## 5. Workplace Behaviour
5.1 RTA maintains a respectful workplace free of harassment and discrimination.
5.2 Health and safety instructions **must** be followed at all times.

## 6. Reporting Misconduct
6.1 Employees **must** report suspected misconduct, fraud, or safety violations through the official whistleblowing channel.
6.2 Good-faith reporters are protected from retaliation.

## 7. Consequences
Breaches of this code may lead to disciplinary action up to and including termination, and referral to authorities where laws are broken.
