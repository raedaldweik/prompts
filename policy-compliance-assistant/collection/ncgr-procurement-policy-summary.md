# Procurement Procedure Summary (Internal)

## Scope
Applies to all procurement of goods, works, and services by the entity
through the Etimad platform, under the Government Tenders and Procurement
Law (GTPL) and its Executive Regulations.

## Methods, in order of preference
1. **Public tender** — the default method. Advertised on Etimad; open to all
   qualified bidders.
2. **Limited tender** — only where the law's conditions are met (e.g. goods
   available only from a limited set of suppliers); invitations documented.
3. **Direct award** — exceptional; only within the value limits and cases the
   Executive Regulations allow, with written justification approved at the
   required authority level.
4. **Framework agreements** — for repetitive needs; call-offs follow the
   framework's terms.

## Key procedural rules (internal policy)
- **§3.1 Planning.** Annual procurement plans are published on Etimad;
  unplanned purchases require a documented justification.
- **§3.4 No splitting.** A requirement **must not be divided** into smaller
  purchases to avoid a tender threshold or an approval level. Suspected
  splitting is reported to the compliance office.
- **§4.2 Advertising periods.** Public tenders remain open for the minimum
  period set by the Executive Regulations for their value band; shortening a
  period requires the stated exceptional grounds, documented.
- **§5.1 Evaluation.** Bids are evaluated only against the published criteria
  by a committee of at least three members with no conflict of interest.
- **§5.6 Single-bid outcomes.** If only one bid is received, the committee
  documents why competition was limited and whether re-tendering is required
  before any award.
- **§6.3 Amendments.** Post-award changes are limited to the percentage caps
  in the Executive Regulations; cumulative amendments beyond the cap require
  a new procurement.
- **§7.2 Records.** All tender documents, evaluations, and approvals are
  retained in Etimad for audit.

## Approval thresholds (illustrative for the demo)
| Award value (SAR) | Approval level |
|---|---|
| < 100,000 | Department head |
| 100,000 – 1,000,000 | Procurement committee |
| 1,000,000 – 5,000,000 | Deputy / authorized officer |
| > 5,000,000 | Head of entity |
