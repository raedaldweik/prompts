# RTA Data Governance Policy (Sample)

**Document ID:** POL-DG-001 · **Version:** 1.0 · **Owner:** Data Management Office · **Effective:** 2026-01-01

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines how RTA data is classified, accessed, shared, and retained to protect privacy, ensure quality, and support data-driven decisions.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all RTA employees, contractors, and third parties who create, access, or process RTA data.

## 3. Data Classification
All data must be classified into one of four levels:
- **Public** — may be shared externally without restriction.
- **Internal** — for use within RTA only.
- **Confidential** — limited to named teams; sharing requires the data owner's approval.
- **Restricted** — highly sensitive (e.g. personal data, security data); access on a strict need-to-know basis only.

## 4. Access Control
4.1 Access to Internal data or above shall be granted on the principle of least privilege.
4.2 Access to Confidential or Restricted data **must** be approved in writing by the data owner and reviewed every six months.
4.3 Access **must** be revoked within 24 hours of an employee changing role or leaving.

## 5. Data Sharing with Third Parties
5.1 Sharing Confidential or Restricted data with any third party **must** be covered by a signed data-sharing agreement reviewed by Legal.
5.2 Personal data **must not** be shared without a lawful basis and the approval of the Data Protection Officer.
5.3 Data shared externally **shall** be limited to the minimum necessary for the stated purpose.

## 6. Data Quality
6.1 Each dataset shall have a named data steward responsible for accuracy and completeness.
6.2 Data quality issues shall be logged and remediated within the agreed service levels.

## 7. Retention & Disposal
7.1 Data shall be retained only as long as required for its purpose or by law.
7.2 Restricted data **must** be securely disposed of once its retention period expires.

## 8. Roles & Responsibilities
- **Data Owner** — accountable for classification and access approvals.
- **Data Steward** — responsible for day-to-day quality.
- **Data User** — must handle data according to its classification.

## 9. Compliance
Breaches of this policy may result in disciplinary action and, where applicable, regulatory penalties. Suspected breaches **must** be reported to the Data Management Office within 48 hours.
